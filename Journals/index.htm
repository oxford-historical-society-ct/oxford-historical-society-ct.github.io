Ledgers of a Young Oxford Man

1851-1856

 

The Oxford Historical Society recently obtained a set of journal ledgers of a resident of Oxford Connecticut during the 1850’s. The entries begin on September 1st, 1851 and end on October 31st of 1856. The original owner’s name is not on the manuscripts but there are several clues.  From the clues within the ledgers, it appears that the writer was Burton Harger, who died in 1861.

 

As each monthly ledger page is transcribed and posted on the Oxford Historical Society Facebook page, they will be posted here as well.
