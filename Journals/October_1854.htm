Burton Harger’s Journal, Oxford, Connecticut

October, 1854

1 Sunday, X

2 Picked 24 1/2 stacks of corn ???? 3 of us boys 88 in all day.

3 Carted stalks. Thrashed carrot seed.

4 Picked up apples over to Moose Hill

5 Finished picking corn. Dug potatoes up to B. lot  Behold!!! found one weighed 1 ½ lbs.

6 Picked quinces. Had 7 barrels

7 Dug potatoes up to the B. lot. Hendricks auction

8 Sunday

9 Carted stalks. Cleaned carrot seed had 30 bls, Lettuce 8 bls.

10 Carted stalks. Dug potatoes & Sun set in a cloud

11 Dug potatoes

12 Started to N. Haven at 2 in the morning got there a 6 1/2 a foot. Saw a pumpkin weighing 160 lbs came out in the cars the same day and got home 9 1/2

13 Dug potatoes. Nearly throug with potatoes

14 Do. "  Rainy

15 Sunday.  A Snow Squall Windy

16 Dug potatoes, finished. Picked up walnuts. Cold

17 Picked up chestnuts. Dug potatoes for Mr. Curtiss

18 Barreled up potatoes. Pulled beets

19 Pulled beets. Picked up walnuts

20 Pulled carrots. Went a hunting caught 3 gray squirrels

21 Went a walnutting. The boys went into the brook

22 Sunday X

23 Hired out to teach school at Barn Hill East Village Monroe

24 Carried Henry down to the cars. Went to Ansonia with potatoes

25 Butchered two hogs. Weighed 131 lbs. on Mr. Nichols’ S. bars.

26 Pulled R. Beggas

27 Do " "

28 Finished the R. Beggas.

29 Sunday

30 Thrashed fox tail seed.

31 Carted muck in the Old Orchard.
